Stop Sexual/Domestic Abuse!

Safetee is a mobile app that seeks to end sexual and domestic abuse by providing users with tools to keep themselves safe in dangerous situations, real-time evidence recording and NGO counseling/prosecution support.

About Safetee

safetee is an android app that keeps you safe and protects you from violence.

safetee helps you quickly contact your close circle incase of emergencies with your exact location, helps you record evidence of sexual/domestic violence and provides you resources to keep you safe at all times.
