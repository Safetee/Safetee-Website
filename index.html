About Safetee

Safetee is a mobile application that keeps you safe and protects you from violence.

Safetee helps you quickly contact your close circle incase of emergencies with your exact location, helps you record evidence of sexual/domestic violence and provides you resources to keep you safe at all times.

In the event sexual/domestic violence occurs, Safetee connects the victim with trusted and reliable NGOs who specialize in treating sexual/domestic violence cases; so the victim can get counselling, rehabilitation and justice.

Safetee is a product of the CHIMEHACK hackathon organised by Co Creation Hub, we won the hackathon and set off on this amazing journey to stop sexual/domestic violence. Interestingly, our team has a majority of males who are passionate about solving this problem of sexual/domestic violence because we believe it's an epidemic that affects us all and must be ended.

Features

Circle of Friends

Add your most trusted friends and family to your cirle so you can notify them easily with your location, to come get you in times of danger. You can add as many friends as you want.

Get Help

Safetee offers a one touch panic button that sends SMS Messages to your Circle of Friends, with your location, in times of danger. You can also specify the type of help required.

Evidence Gathering

Safetee provides encrypted evidence gathering tools for real-time evidence gathering using audio recording and third party reporting using media files. Evidence can be used to convict offenders.

Trusted NGOs

Don't keep quiet! Safetee partners with government registered NGOs who can help you in Sexual/Domestic Violence cases. NGOs provide support to victims and help to prosecute offenders.
